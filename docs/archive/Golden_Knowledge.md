# FUNDCHAIN GOLDEN KNOWLEDGE BASE (USER-FACING)

## 💰 MONEY, TOKENS & SWAPPING
- **How do I get started with money?** To start, connect your Phantom wallet. You need SOL. You then swap your SOL for FC (FundChain Tokens). 1 SOL always gives you 100 FC.
- **Can I turn FC back into SOL?** Yes. You can withdraw or swap your FC tokens back into SOL at the same 1:100 rate at any time, provided they aren't currently locked in an active investment.
- **What are the costs/fees?** We use the Solana network, so "gas fees" are nearly zero (less than a cent). FundChain does not charge hidden fees for investing.

## 🛡️ SAFETY, SECURITY & SCAMS
- **Is my investment safe? Is it a scam?** We use "Milestone-Based Escrow." This means your money is NOT given to the creator immediately. It is locked in a smart contract. The creator only gets paid when they finish a specific part of the project and the investors approve it.
- **What if the project is a rug pull or the creator disappears?** If a creator stops working, the remaining money stays in the escrow contract. It cannot be taken by the creator. In these cases, the platform can trigger a refund of the remaining tokens to the investors.
- **How does the AI Scam Detector work?** Our AI analyzes every project. It looks for copied descriptions (plagiarism), checks the creator's wallet history, and looks for "scam patterns." It gives a Risk Score: Low, Medium, or High.

## 🗳️ VOTING & MILESTONES
- **How do milestones work?** A project is split into 3-5 parts (milestones). When a creator finishes a part, they upload proof.
- **Why do I need to vote?** Your vote releases the money. If you aren't happy with the proof the creator showed, you vote "No." If the majority votes "Yes," the creator gets their next payout.
- **How many votes do I have?** 1 FC token equals 1 vote. The more you invest, the more power you have in the decision-making.

## 🚀 FOR PROJECT CREATORS
- **How do I get my funding?** You must define clear milestones. You only receive funds as you complete these milestones and your investors approve your work.
- **Why is my risk score high?** Risk scores are high if your profile is new, your description is too short, or your roadmap is vague. Be detailed to lower your risk.
